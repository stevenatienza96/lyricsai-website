Features

Everything you need for Sunday morning

Built for worship leaders, AV teams, and pastors who want fast setup and polished on-screen lyrics.

🎵

Song search

Find complete song lyrics online with AI. Section markers like [Verse 1] and [Chorus] are added automatically so slides split cleanly.

📖

Bible

Search scripture and generate slides with verse references at the bottom. Supports NIV, ESV, KJV, NKJV, NLT, and Ang Magandang Balita (AMB).

📝

Sermon notes

Paste outline notes and let AI build a slide deck — including auto-fetched scripture for references it detects in your text.

✏️

Slide editor

Fine-tune lyrics line by line, insert section markers, and save songs to your catalog for reuse across services.

✨

AI slide generation

Paste or import text and generate presentation-ready slides (max two lines each) with theme-aware animations and transitions.

🎬

Backgrounds & video

Import images and looping video backgrounds. Apply a background to one slide or the entire queue in one click.

🖥️

Dual-screen live

Control window on your laptop, full-screen output on the projector. Navigate with arrow keys, black screen, and a live preview panel.

📚

Catalog & queue

Build a service order with multiple songs, open saved projects, and keep a searchable song catalog for your ministry.

🔑

Subscription licensing

Activate once with your license key. OpenRouter AI access is provisioned for you — no API keys to manage on Sunday.

Workflow

From search to live in minutes

  1. 1

    Download & activate

    Install LyricsAI and enter the license key you received. AI is ready immediately.

  2. 2

    Import content

    Search a song, pull a Bible passage, or paste sermon notes — then generate slides.

  3. 3

    Style the queue

    Reorder slides, pick backgrounds, adjust animations, and save to your catalog.

  4. 4

    Go live

    Select your output display, hit Go Live, and present with keyboard shortcuts.
